Grapple Ascent
Grapple Ascent was made in Unity in about 96 hours for GMTK Game Jam 2024. This was one of my first game jams and was fun to collaborate with 3 other developers

Rope Rendering

A rope was needed to use as the grapple string, this started by using the default line renderer in unity. It worked well until the line had to curve around objects and the line cut off. To fix this i created a 3D version of the line renderer that takes in a mesh and still uses line renderer as a base to know where the line will start and end. I also made the line be a bit more strict on where it could connect.

Camera Controller

The Camera controller collides with walls and obstacles to not clip into them. I though the game lacked some kind of response to the player that you are going fast. It did not feel good to swing fast so when you speed up your FOV increases and you get a trail behind you.
