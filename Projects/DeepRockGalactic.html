FOG
Deep Rock Galactic is created by Ghost Ship Games and made in Unreal Engine 4. In my time at Invisible walls i got the amazing opertunity to work on this great game by creating a full perk damage modifier system and a perk for the system.

Perk Damage Modifier System

Before my work only a small amount of damage modifier perks were in the game which made their implmentations simple and Temporary. In the backend we have a list of healthtype that lines up with a list of modifiers and thresholds to modify damage. When damaging something it checks the healthtype of the target to know damage modifiers.

In the front end where the designer balances these damage modifiers they use the Unreal Editor and a cuastom struct i created.

Finish Them PERK

The finish them perk makes your damage multiply by X amount when the enemy is below X% damage. The thresholds are different for small and big enemies to not make the perk too over powered.

The perk used global stat values originally for damage modifier and thresholds wich made it very specific for the perk at hand. To scale it better with the games future, the damage modifier system was made like talked about above.

Currently multiplying damage by 4 times when the enemy has 50% health left, These values are exagurated for show.
