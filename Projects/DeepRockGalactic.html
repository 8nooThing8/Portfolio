Deep Rock Galactic
Deep Rock Galactic is created by Ghost Ship Games and made in Unreal Engine 4. In my time at Invisible walls i got the amazing opertunity to work on this great game by creating a full perk damage modifier system and a perk for the system.

Perk Damage Modifier System

Before my work only a small amount of damage modifier perks were in the game which made their implmentations simple and Temporary. I had to create a system that you can rely on and is abstract enough to be used alot in the future.
How it works is in the backend we have a container for general damage modifiers that has a condition and a multiplier that can be overwritten. All the developer have to do is make a damage modifier and add it to the list with your own condition and multiplier when the modifier should be active.
In the front end where the designer balances these damage modifiers its much easier to understand, they use the Unreal Editor and a custom struct i created.

Finish Them PERK

The "Finish Them" perk makes your damage multiply by X amount when the enemy is below X% damage. The thresholds are different for small and big enemies to not make the perk too over powered.

The perk originaly had to use global values for damage modifier and thresholds which made it unsafe if you accidently edit it directly and made not much sense anyway. To scale it better with the games future, the damage modifier system was made like talked about above.

Currently multiplying damage by 4 times when the enemy has less than 50% health left, These values are exagurated for show.
